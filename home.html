About this firm

[ 01.Z · FAQ ] PRE-APPLICATION

Frequently asked

What founders ask before they apply.

What does HIMARK do?

HIMARK is a premium strategic growth consultancy. The firm designs and operates the brand, demand, and AI infrastructure beneath founder-led firms ready to compound. It works with the rigour of a management consultancy and the agility of a founder office.

Where is HIMARK based?

HIMARK is headquartered in Randburg, Gauteng, South Africa. The firm serves clients across South Africa, Sub-Saharan Africa, and remotely worldwide.

Is HIMARK an agency or a consultancy?

A consultancy. HIMARK does not pitch on RFPs, does not bid on price, and does not take work that does not sharpen the firm edge. Engagements are scoped against the client trajectory, not a fixed menu.

How much does HIMARK cost?

Engagements start from R 50,000 per month. The firm operates three retainer tiers — Signature Partner, Growth Partner, and Private Partner (by invitation only). Standalone service mandates are quoted separately. Exact pricing is shared after an Atlas intake.

How do I work with HIMARK?

By application. Applications run through Atlas, the firm principal-trained assistant, or via a scheduled 30-minute discovery session with a principal. Every application is reviewed by a principal within five working days.

Who runs my engagement?

A senior principal personally anchors every HIMARK engagement. There are no junior accounts. The principal who reads your application is the principal who runs your engagement.

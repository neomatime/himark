Open channels

Use the right channel for the right purpose. Formal engagement enquiries go through Intake; everything else routes through one of the addresses below. Every enquiry is read by a HIMARK principal.

General Enquiries

For general questions about HIMARK, the firm's approach, or anything that doesn't fit the other channels. Read by the principal office; response within five working days.

Press & Media

For journalists, editors, podcast hosts, and event organisers requesting commentary, interviews, or the firm's perspective on a market matter. Include outlet, brief, and deadline.

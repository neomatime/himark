03.A · INTRODUCTIONMETHOD / 01METHODOLOGY · DEFINED
[ 03.A.01 ]

A process built
for precision.

Every HIMARK engagement follows the same disciplined sequence. Not because we lack flexibility — but because rigour is a precondition for results. We do not skip steps. We do not abbreviate diagnosis. We do not move to execution before the architecture is sound.

03.B · THE FOUR-STEP SEQUENCEMETHOD / 0204 · PHASES
PHASE [ 01 ]
01
Diagnostic

A structured commercial audit that surfaces real growth constraints — not symptoms. No assumptions. No pre-built frameworks applied before understanding is complete.

[ DURATION ]

2–3 weeks. Includes leadership interviews, financial review, market analysis, and competitive landscape assessment.

PHASE [ 02 ]
02
Architecture

Growth strategy designed from first principles — revenue model, go-to-market, positioning, and the operational stack required to sustain it.

[ OUTPUT ]

Strategic growth blueprint. Prioritised initiative roadmap. Resource and technology requirements. Board-ready presentation.

PHASE [ 03 ]
03
Execution

We embed with your leadership to drive implementation through weekly accountability loops and measurable milestones.

[ CADENCE ]

Weekly execution reviews. Bi-weekly leadership alignment. Monthly board update. Ongoing advisory access per engagement tier.

PHASE [ 04 ]
04
Optimise

Quarterly performance reviews with real metrics. Strategy adapts to market conditions. What works is scaled. What does not is cut.

[ METRICS ]

Revenue growth velocity. Pipeline conversion rate. Market share movement. Strategic initiative completion. ROI against engagement cost.

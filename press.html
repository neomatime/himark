HIMARK is a premium strategic growth consultancy headquartered in Randburg, Gauteng, working with founder-led businesses pursuing premium-tier market positions across South Africa and globally. Founded in 2026 by Neo Matime and Neo Mokgwadi, the firm operates on a deliberately limited mandate model — a small number of engagements each quarter, every one anchored by a senior principal. HIMARK designs and operates the strategic infrastructure beneath the businesses it works with: brand architecture, demand engineering, AI integration through its proprietary AIRaaS platform, and direct principal counsel. The firm does not pitch, does not chase volume, and accepts engagements by application. Its founding doctrine is that clarity precedes scale, volume is a tax on quality, and architecture compounds where campaigns expire.

09.B · USAGEPRESS / 07PERMITTED · NOT PERMITTED

[ 07 ] Usage

Guidelines for editorial use.

The HIMARK wordmark, glyph, and visual identity may be used freely in editorial contexts — articles, features, news reporting, conference materials. Commercial use requires written authorisation.

[ 09.B.07 ] GUIDELINES · ×6
+PERMITTED

Editorial coverage

Use the wordmark, glyph, and any provided asset freely in articles, features, news reports, and journalistic coverage of the firm or its principals.

+PERMITTED

Conference & event materials

When a HIMARK principal participates in a speaking engagement or panel, the wordmark and bio may be used on event programmes, slides, and venue collateral.

+PERMITTED

Citation & attribution

When citing the firm, "HIMARK" or "HIMARK (Pty) Ltd" are both correct. Do not pluralise. Always capitalise in full.

×NOT PERMITTED

Modified marks

Do not recolour, stretch, distort, outline, or add effects to the wordmark or glyph. Use the supplied files without alteration. Mark sizes below 64px should use the SVG.

×NOT PERMITTED

Endorsement implication

The HIMARK mark may not be used in a way that implies endorsement, partnership, or affiliation that does not exist. Always represent the relationship accurately.

×NOT PERMITTED

Commercial & merchandise

The HIMARK identity may not be applied to commercial products, merchandise, or paid advertising without prior written authorisation from the firm.

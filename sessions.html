08.A · COUNSELCOUNSEL / 01NINETY · MINUTES
[ 08.A.01 ] PRINCIPAL · LED

For when the
constraint is unclear.

A Strategic Advisory Session is a contained, ninety-minute conversation with one of HIMARK's principals — convened specifically around the constraint you are trying to break. Not a sales call. Not a discovery meeting. A working session.

You bring the brief: the position you hold, the move you are weighing, the question you cannot yet answer cleanly. A principal brings the firm's apparatus — frameworks, market read, sector pattern recognition — and works with you to sharpen the brief into a course of action.

You leave with a written one-page summary: the constraint as the principal heard it, the angles worth pursuing, and the prerequisites for each. Useful whether or not a formal engagement follows.

Convened by
